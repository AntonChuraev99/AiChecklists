AI checklist app: questions, answered

Can AI make a checklist for me? expand_more

Yes. Gisti's AI turns your input — a photo, PDF, link, typed text, or voice — into a structured checklist in seconds, then lets you edit any item.

How do I turn a photo into a checklist? expand_more

Open Gisti, tap to add a photo of any list, note, or document, and the AI reads it and builds a checklist you can check off right away.

Can I turn a PDF or a link into a checklist? expand_more

Yes. Drop in a PDF or paste a web link and Gisti's AI extracts the steps and organizes them into a ready-to-use checklist.

Is there a free AI to-do list app? expand_more

Gisti is free to start — 5 checklists, 5 AI fills each, 10 AI credits a day, and 10 recurring reminders. No sign-up needed to try it.

Does Gisti work on both Android and the web? expand_more

Yes. Gisti has the same features on Android and the web, and your checklists sync through the cloud so they're the same on every device.

How much does Gisti Premium cost? expand_more

Premium is $1.99/month and unlocks unlimited checklists and fills, 300 AI credits a day, Google Calendar export, and a weekly digest.
